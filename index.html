About Us

We are the Utah Unicycling Society, a group of Utah-based unicyclers who are dedicated to the pursuit of perfecting our sport, while doing what we love. We hope to inspire this same level of commitment and dedication to others in Utah. In this site, you can find resources to help further learn, meet new people, and above all, have fun!

While our primary location is in Park City UT, we encourage all residents of Utah to join. Currently, there are no "meet-ups" or any other obligations, but we hope to soon change that.

Join Us

Today, millions of people are realizing that unicycling is great. Join or follow the Utah Unicycling Society, and become a better unicyler today. In order to have more fun unicycling, we need you to join. All ages, and all Utah residents are welcome to join for free.

Find Us

The Utah Unicycling Society currently has no location, as it is a social construct. While we would like to change this, you can always find us digitaly. Follow us @UtahUnicyclingSociety on most social media sites, or contact us at our email.
